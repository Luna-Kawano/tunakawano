Hi, I'm Tuna.

0:00
profile.jpg

Imagine this. January 2023, you're leisurely reading in a hammock. Suddenly, out of absolutely nowhere, a HUGE wooden pole holding the hammock falls and squashes your poor little noggin...

I know what you're thinking. What does this have to do with literally anything? Well, in a nutshell, this is how my "I started selling my art" phase officially started (gasp).

I'm a self-taught artist from Banff, Canada, and I've been arting away for as long as I can remember. Until I hit my head, I never thought I would sell my art, but the universe works in a funny way sometimes. Art has made having a concussion more bearable and I hope my work brings some joy into your life as well🫶

If you ever have questions about my art or want to chat about concussion things, pls feel free to reach out!

Much love,
Tuna🐟
