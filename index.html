I bonked my head + now I'm sellin some artsie fartsies

Thank you thank you thank you for visiting my page!!! Without further ado, here's my story:

Imagine this. January 2023, I was leisurely reading on a hammock at an Airbnb. Suddenly, out of absolutely nowhere, the HUGE wooden pole holding the hammock falls and squashes my poor little noggin. Ever since then, my brain has been a silly thing and doing normal everyday things has been quite a struggle (rip me). 

I know what you're thinking. What does this have to do with literally anything? Well, in a nutshell, this is how my "I started selling my art" phase officially started (gasp). 

In terms of some art-relevant content, I'm a self-taught artist from Banff, Canada, and I like to sketch, paint, and use any medium as I chaotically try to find my style. I've been arting for as long as I can remember but I never imagined I'd start selling my art until I hit my head—the universe works in a funny way sometimes...Art has definitely made having a concussion more bearable and I hope my work brings some joy to your life as well🫶

If you ever have questions about my art or want to chat about concussion things, pls feel free to reach out!
